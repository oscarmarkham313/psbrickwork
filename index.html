Common Questions

Everything

to know.

Not sure where to start? Here are the questions we hear most often.

We start with a free site visit to measure your space and understand your vision. From there we produce a full photorealistic 3D render — showing every brick, every finish, every dimension — before any work begins. You review it, we refine it, then we build exactly what you approved.
Every build is bespoke so pricing varies by size, materials and complexity. A standalone Mago oven typically starts from €4,000. Full outdoor kitchen suites start from €8,000. We'll give you a detailed quote after the free consultation with no obligation.
A Mago oven build typically takes 3–5 days on site. A full outdoor kitchen suite can take 1–3 weeks depending on size and complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline during the design phase so you can plan around it.
Absolutely. All materials are specifically chosen for weather resistance. We use frost-proof tiles, sealed stone, stainless steel hardware and weather-rated plasters. Our builds are designed to look exceptional year-round — rain or shine.
We currently serve Newry & Dundalk, Co. Louth & Meath, and North Dublin. Not sure if we cover your area? Get in touch and we'll let you know — we do occasionally take on projects outside our main service zone.
Yes, completely. We visit your site, measure the space, listen to your ideas and then follow up with a 3D concept. There's no charge and no obligation — we only take on projects where we're confident we can deliver something exceptional.
